## 5. ⭐ THE STRATEGY BAKE-OFF — findings (this is the heart of the next phase)
`backend/research/strategy_lab.py` competes alpha sources on the REAL Brent curve (2016-2026), strict no-look-ahead, variable cost. Run it: `py -3.11 -m backend.research.strategy_lab`.

**Results (NET Sharpe @ $0.02/RT, robust across params/windows/3 eras 2016-19/2020-22/2023-26):**
| Strategy | Net Sharpe | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| momentum | −2.0 | REJECTED — spreads don't trend |
| carry | −1.8 | REJECTED — it's the losing mirror of mean-rev |
| Calendar/term spreads MR | ~0.18 | weak — nearly efficient |
| **Butterfly RV (discrete hold-to-revert, front-curve flies)** | **+1.21** | 🏆 **THE EDGE** — PF 1.9, tiny DD, ~4× incumbent |
| Regime-conditional (incumbent) | ~0.3 | benchmark to beat |
| Everything equal-weight | ~0.30 | junk dilutes gold |
| Top-5 by conviction | ~0.54 | concentrating helps |
| + WTI + Brent-WTI arb (42 inst) | ~0.63 | arb diversifies (2020 era +1.13) |

**Key facts established:**
- **Energy curve spreads MEAN-REVERT, they don't trend** (momentum/carry negative).
- **The robust edge is BUTTERFLY (curvature) relative value**, and it **concentrates in the FRONT of the curve** (fly_1 = c1−2c2+c3 alone is Sharpe **+1.35**; deferred flies ≈ efficient). Economic reason: front = physical storage/delivery driven → mean-reverts; back = financialized → efficient.
- **Discrete hold-to-revert** (enter |z|≥1.5, hold until |z|≤0.25) is what preserves the edge net of cost — continuous tanh sizing over-trades (turnover 0.05→0.014, Sharpe survives cost).
- **Parameter/window robustness is excellent** (Sharpe +1.1..+1.3 across all entry/exit/window choices — not overfit).
- **Calendar spreads are weak**; equal-weighting them with flies dilutes Sharpe (1.21→0.30). A pro allocates risk to the edge.

**LOCKED final design = "PULSE RV Desk" — 3 parallel risk sleeves (one engine):**
1. **Butterfly RV** 🏆 — discrete hysteresis band on all front-curve flies (Brent + WTI). Flagship, ~Sharpe 1.2.
2. **Term-Structure RV** — calendar + wider term spreads + **Brent-WTI arb**, conviction/top-N selected. Diversifier (~0.5, shines in dislocations).
3. **Regime (incumbent)** — old regime-conditional book on the 6 core spreads (~0.3). The benchmark we beat.
Together they trade the whole complex (flies/spreads/arb) but you SEE which sleeve earns its risk. WTI is real in the live feed, synth in history (flag it).
